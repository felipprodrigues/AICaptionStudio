## English

1. What does the Transformers library (by Hugging Face) do?

A: It makes it easier to use pre-trained AI models.

---

2. A drawback of running public models locally is:

A: Need for specialized hardware.

---

3. What task does the 'text-generation' pipeline perform?

A: Text generation.

---

4. The temperature parameter controls:

A: The randomness of the generation.

---

5. What does a model with "Q4" in its name mean?

A: Quantized with 4 bits.

---

6. What indicates that a model was fine-tuned to follow instructions?

A: Presence of "instruct" in the name.

---

7. The use of a sidecar in AI applications serves to:

A: Isolate the model from the main application.

---

8. What does model quantization allow?

A: Reduce its memory size.

---

9. The main advantage of using public AI models instead of APIs like ChatGPT is:

A: Free and full control over the model.

---

10. A "distil" model is characterized by:

A: Being a distilled and smaller version.

---

11. The term "mixture of experts" refers to:

A: An architecture with multiple experts.

---

12. What should be considered when choosing a model to run locally?

A: Size in gigabytes.

---

13. True or False? Public models are not pre-trained.

A: False.
